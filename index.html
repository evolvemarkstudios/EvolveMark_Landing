What is STIGMA?

STIGMA is a mobile-first action RPG where you evolve not by grinding levels—but by making bold, irreversible choices. Fight using gesture-based swipes, unlock a living tattoo system that grows with you, and carve your legend through a dynamic morality path. Every build is unique. Every mark has meaning.

Key Features

  • Gesture Combat – Swipe, parry, and combo through cinematic battles
  • Stigma Evolution System – Skill tree that mutates based on your moral choices
  • Reactive Visual Identity – Your character’s form, aura, and weapons change dynamically
  • No Pay-to-Win – Customization-focused monetization only
